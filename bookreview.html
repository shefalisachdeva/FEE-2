Robert Kiyosakis Rich Dad, Poor Dad was in my bookshelf for a long time like an abandoned soul finding solace. It took me one whole year to pick up the book and start reading it, and then the game changed. Lots of reviews and suggestions had given me the opinion of Rich Dad, Poor Dad as a best self-help books of all time, but since I was someone who is reluctant for self-help and more into fiction, I could not possibly have read it. But as soon as I finished the book, I regretted it.
